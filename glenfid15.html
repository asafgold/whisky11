Glenfiddich

  • Single malt
  • Scotland
  • 14 Years old

The Glenfiddich 14 Year Old is a uniquely aged single malt that has been aged in European, American and fresh American oak before going into a 'Solera System' similar to those used for the ageing of quality Sherry. Once aged the whisky is married for a further period in Portuguese oak tuns.Â
